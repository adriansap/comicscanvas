make your own comics on the fly!

Use our app to make comicbook frames and then make animated comic strips! Here's how you make one: 1) Choose a template 2) Start drawing! 3) When you're ready to make a new frame right click on your drawing, save it and click on the 'new frame' btn. 4) You will see slight outline of your previous drawing to make animation easier by having a reference point. 5) Repeat step 3 as many times as you want. 6) Go over to a site like ezgif.com and load your frames to make an animated comicbook strip!
